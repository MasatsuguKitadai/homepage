An evaluation method for measuring SPL and mode shape of tire cavity resonance by using multi-microphone system

Yohsuke Tanaka, Shogo Horikawa and Shigeru Murata
Applied Acoustics
April 2016, Vol.105:171-178, doi:10.1016/j.apacoust.2015.12.009



Research results

This paper presents a new developed evaluation method for tire cavity resonance. Pressure distribution of a tire cavity resonance is experimentally measured by using this method. It is expected that numerical and theoretical models can be confirmed by this proposed method. Acoustic vibration is generated by speaker.

Multi-Microphone System

This experimental facility can measure a pressure distribution in a tire cavity by 8 microphone sensors. Pressured tire is loaded with 400 kN by a hydraulic jack.
