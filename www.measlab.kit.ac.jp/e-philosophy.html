Philosophy of research and education

We contribute education and research of applied optical measurements to market creation and development.

To create and develop industries, it is important to measure all phenomena of Monozukuri (manufacturing industry). Our laboratory promotes measurements of the phenomena through research and development of applied optical, acoustical and fluid measurement. Moreover, we make research and education to create and develop industries, and fruits of research are applied to the benefit of society by patenting and academic-industrial collaboration.
